STEP 5 · TARGET COSTING
💰 FORMULA (Appendix p.114) C* = P × (1 − Mr) × (1 − Mm)
Start from your retail price. Subtract the retailer margin (30%), then the manufacturer margin (40%). The result is your maximum allowable manufacturing cost.
